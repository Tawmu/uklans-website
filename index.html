Our Objectives

UK LANs is a working group formed by organisers and technical teams from UK LAN party events. We work together to improve the quality of events across the country.

Community

Connecting event organisers across the UK to share knowledge and support each other in running successful LAN events.

Technical

Sharing technical expertise around networking, hardware, software, and infrastructure for the betterment of all events.

Standards

Working together to develop and promote best practices that help deliver consistent, high-quality experiences for attendees.
