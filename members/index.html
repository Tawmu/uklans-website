Who's involved?

UK LANs includes people from a wide spectrum of events. From the largest UK LAN party, to the smallest gathering of friends at a village hall.

Everyone involved is here to work together for the better of the community - we leave inter-LAN politics at the door, and do our best to help every LAN run as well as it can.
